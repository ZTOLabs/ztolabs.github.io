How we hire?

Round 1
Online Screening Interview
Online | 1 hour
Get to know each other and discuss relevant experience, skills & knowledge based on your CV.
Round 2
In-Depth Interview
Office / Online | 2 hours
This discussion includes 2 sessions:
  • Competency: Delve into your technical skills & knowledge to see how they align with the applied position.
  • Culture Fit: Meet different ZTO Labs members to check if we get along well and share similar values (This session will happen, if and only if you pass the Competency Interview).
