Technologies

Frontend

Our frontend tech stack is optimized for modern web and mobile app development. Leveraging React, JavaScript, TypeScript, Jest, Cypress, and HTML/CSS, we ensure robust, scalable, and user-friendly applications.

Backend

Our backend tech stack is geared towards robust application development. Utilizing Python (FastAPI), MySQL, Weaviate, SQLAlchemy, and Langchain, we ensure efficient and intelligent backend solutions.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure is pivotal, ensuring seamless operation and scalability of systems. At ZTO Labs, we choose:

Server: Linux on AWS

Database: MySQL, MongoDB, Redis, DynamoDB, Vector Databases

Development Tools

In our development toolkit, we rely on efficient and widely-used tools to streamline our workflow:

  • IDE: Pycharm, VSCode
  • Version Control Systems (VCS): , Github
  • CI/CD: Github Actions

Specialty

We're experts in every phase of product development: building from scratch, scaling globally, and even refactoring entire systems to meet evolving industry demands.

Scratch Builder

Our name, ZTO, stands for Zero To One, reflecting our commitment to helping businesses build impactful products from scratch. We specialize in transforming raw ideas into world-class products. Whatever the idea, we outline a clear roadmap and leverage the latest technologies and top talent to bring it to life.

System Revive

We are experts at updating old systems to meet modern tech standards. For example, in our project with PhotoStudy, we upgraded their entire codebase from PHP to Python and JavaScript. This upgrade fixed technical problems and prepared the system for future growth. The new PhotoStudy app, launched after six months, now performs better and offers a great user experience.

Global Scaling

We turn great ideas into global successes by finding market opportunities, anticipating needs, quickly launching minimum viable products (MVPs), and continuously improving them based on user feedback. Our approach has helped our partners succeed, like PhotoStudy, which started as a simple idea and now helps millions of students in over 250 U.S. schools.
